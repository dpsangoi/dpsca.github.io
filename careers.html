Our Hiring Philosophy


We invest in building our team first. While others focus on building their client base, at DPS we ensure we have competent, qualified, enthusiastic personnel on board before we accept new clients. As a result, we are continuously recruiting, taking on strategic hires, and integrating ourselves within the community.

We don’t hire skill sets. We hire based on aptitude, attitude and commitment—so you can be sure the people you bring on board from DPS will be as enthusiastic about doing your work, as you are.


Essentials of working with DPS


  Aspire to do more than just number crunching
  No desire to sleep in office formals
  Attitude to getting things done
  Appetite to learn and grow
